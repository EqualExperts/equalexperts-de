Empowerment and Tech

Becky Smith

|

Sat Feb 29 2020

Empowerment and Tech is a community of women in Berlin, that represent the diversity of roles within the Tech Industry. It is important to get more women into IT, but equally important to highlight the broad range of roles and expertise needed throughout the tech lifecycle. Our females represent HR, Sales, PR, Product, Coders and many many more.

What started as an experiment has turned into a small but growing success.

We bring a wealth of expertise and knowledge together in a safe place - where we can help learn from and empower each other. We discuss a variety of topics, share our experiences and advice. Usually, we come away having learnt something and met someone new. 

This is a forum to ask questions we might otherwise be scared to ask, to lean on expertise for some creative solutions, or hear someone talk about a war story. The format is always casual, there are no speakers as such - just a female-only sharing place.  

Topics discussed so far :

Mentorships  

Having a Female Boss  

Interrupting bias without causing offence 

How to get more females into roles like ours 

Am I in IT if I am not technical? 

Between events we also have a slack community where we keep in touch, share links and roles. The events are quarterly and sponsored by different companies. A big thank you to www.kommitment.works and www.equalexperts.de who have helped us thus far.

If you are interested in joining, or sponsoring the next event please get in touch.
